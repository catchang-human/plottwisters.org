Hey! Welcome to our studio.

Plot Twisters is a team of researchers, designers, and technologists that creates online opportunities for self-reflection and digital literacy, especially in K-12 communities. We work with students, educators, and organizations that serve young people.

We make digital tools to support young people as they build their journeys.

Most of our time is spent researching in our Think Tank and designing online experiences. We create role model reports, design self-reflection activities, and mentor students as they explore their strengths and values, in career and life.

Our studio officially launched in January 2020 in North America. Today, our team is spread over three continents and six timezones.

Good times with our

Project Partners

The EMPOWER Lab

The EMPOWER Lab at the University of Georgia researches interpersonal and racial trauma in the Black community. Together, we're developing an online psychoeducation experience for youth healing from racial trauma called the Racial Trauma CARE Package.

Let's Talk LD

Let's Talk LD gathers and trains educators dedicated to neurodiversity. Young neurodiverse students often lack access to resources for personal and professional development. We collaborate regularly with their leadership as we build our tools, to ensure our approach creates opportunities for students with learning, social communication, and attentional differences.

verynice

verynice, in addition to being a social enterprise and design strategy studio, publishes educational toolkits about creativity, design, and teamwork. Currently, we're collaborating on a creative journey building toolkit for students to reflect on their lifestyle and career goals, to publish in 2021.
